About me

Joel D Harper

Austin, Texas

I grew up in Pullman, WA and was raised a "farm kid". After graduating from Washington State University in 2001 I moved to Seattle and began working for a company that manufactured personal care items, where I got to learn about Product Manufacturing, Marketing and Entrepreneurship.

I made the decision to move to Austin, TX in 2006 and found my home, my wife and began to plant my Texas roots. My wife and I had our first child last May and Grace Anderson Harper is proud to be a native Austinite.

I love the process of taking a dream or an idea and making it a reality. I enjoy creating and delivering high quality experiences and products.

Learning, education and the process of ongoing improvement is important to me. I've always been a driven person but becoming a Dad has certainly impacted my motivation to be the best person I can be.
