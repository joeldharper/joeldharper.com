About me

Joel D Harper

Austin, Texas

  • Biography
  • What is my blog about

I grew up in Pullman, WA and have been active in athletics my entire life as well as being raised a "farm kid". After graduating from Washington State University in 2001 I moved to Seattle and began working for a company that manufactured personal care items, learning the in's and out's of Project Management and Entrepreneurship.

I made the decision to move to Austin, TX in 2006 and found my home, my wife and began to plant my Texas roots. My wife and I had our first child last May and Grace Anderson Harper is proud to be a native Austinite. I like to spend my time training at Atomic Athlete and running the trails around Town Lake.
